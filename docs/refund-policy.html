Refund Policy Scaffold

The decisions DTC must approve before accepting online payment. This page is not a final return or refund offer.

Not an approved policy

Online checkout is not active. Eligibility windows, fees, exclusions and refund timing have not been approved. Do not rely on this scaffold as authorization to return an item.

1. Current status

DTC currently confirms purchases and service work through written quotes, order acknowledgements, invoices, repair authorizations and applicable warranty documents. Those transaction-specific terms control. Contact DTC before shipping any item back.

2. Existing quote and service orders

For an existing order, contact DTC with the order or invoice number, part number, reason for the request, item condition, and relevant photos or test information. DTC will identify the written return, cancellation or warranty terms that apply. Sending an item without authorization does not establish return eligibility.

Warranty service and a discretionary return are separate. The written warranty supplied for the product or service controls warranty coverage and remedy.

3. Decisions required before checkout launch

An authorized DTC owner and qualified counsel must approve and publish:

  • which product conditions and services are returnable, including brand new, certified new, refurbished, used, exchange, special-order and repaired-customer units;
  • the request window and how it is measured, required return authorization, inspection/acceptance criteria, and the address/process for returns;
  • non-returnable items, opened/installed/energized items, altered items, missing components, ESD or shipping damage, and custom/special-order exceptions;
  • cancellation timing before and after payment, testing, procurement or shipment;
  • restocking or inspection fees, original and return shipping, insurance, duties and who bears each cost;
  • how wrong, damaged, defective, lost and late shipments are handled and how carrier claims interact with customer remedies;
  • refund method, authorization levels, partial refunds, PayPal fee treatment where lawful, processing targets and customer communications;
  • interaction with the applicable warranty, purchase orders, credit terms, chargebacks and non-waivable legal rights.

4. Safe operational process to implement

The production process should create a case tied to the immutable order record, verify the requester, record approval authority, issue an RMA when required, document inspection, and use a stable refund idempotency key. A timeout or unclear PayPal response must be reconciled before another refund is attempted.

Refunds should be sent only to the original payment method unless an authorized, documented exception is legally and operationally approved. Customer wording must distinguish “refund requested,” “refund submitted,” and “refund completed”; bank or payment-provider posting time should not be presented as a DTC guarantee.

5. Contact

For a current order, email info@dtc247.com or call 201-244-6477 before returning anything. Include the order reference and part number.
