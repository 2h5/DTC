Our Company

Our Story

We built DTC as a direct channel between plant engineers and the technical team doing the work—removing third-party layers when a critical control system needs a clear answer.

Since 2018, the mission has stayed focused: deliver component-level support that helps plants save time, control cost and keep essential equipment operating.

Engineering Leadership

Our Founders

Founding engineer Harikumar spent years as a chief control system engineer, developing the lab and test procedures behind component-level repair. He was later joined by James T, who brought more than 15 years of control system engineering experience.

The company was shaped around a simple operating principle: the person diagnosing the board should be accessible to the plant team depending on it.
