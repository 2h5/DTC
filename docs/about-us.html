Our Company

Engineers first, everything else second

Direct Turbine Controls was formed in 2018 by control system engineers who wanted to work directly with plants, not through a call center. That's still how we operate.

Built by the engineers who repair your boards

Our founding engineer, Harikumar, spent years as chief control system engineer at a controls solutions company, building the lab and test procedures that made component-level repair possible in the first place. He was later joined by James T, who brought 15+ years of his own control system engineering experience.

Working across the industry, it became clear that as companies started prioritizing margins over quality, there was room for one that didn't. Direct Turbine Controls was formed as a direct channel between plant engineers and the technical team actually doing the repair, cutting out the third-party layers and the fees that come with them.
