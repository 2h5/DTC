Technical Difference

Reasons to Choose Us

Six practical differences in the way DTC diagnoses, repairs and validates turbine control equipment.

01 / EXPERIENCE

20+ years of experience in Control System board repairs

Our unparalleled expertise and hands-on proficiency in both Control System and Circuit Board component-level repair set us apart from a generic electronics shop.

02 / DIAGNOSTICS

Advanced diagnostics

We go beyond surface-level troubleshooting, using real-time monitoring and data analysis to pinpoint root causes and build repair solutions tailored to the actual failure, not just the symptom.

03 / COMPONENTS

Top quality replacement parts

We use industrial-standard components for quality and reliability, and specialize in manufacturing obsolete components when they can no longer be sourced.

04 / VALIDATION

Functional testing

Every unit that leaves DTC undergoes thorough functional testing on live control panels to confirm it meets requirements before it ships.

05 / RESPONSE

Fast service

Deep experience and ready stock of components significantly reduce turnaround time. Our in-house lab runs state-of-the-art equipment and live control panels, ask about same-day repair.

06 / ASSURANCE

Warranty exceeding industry standards

Alongside every other test, each unit undergoes a stress and soak test to confirm it's functional under real operating conditions, giving us the confidence to back it with an industry-leading warranty.

Before It Ships

Functional Testing

Every unit that leaves DTC undergoes functional testing on live control panels. Stress and soak testing adds evidence that the repair will operate under real conditions—not only on a static bench.
