What We Do

Services

Testing, repair, spare parts and remanufacturing for turbine, generator and excitation control systems — GE Speedtronic, Bently Nevada, Woodward, Rolls-Royce Entronics and more.

Direct Turbine's repair facility tests, repairs, certifies and remanufactures control panel cards and parts for the plants that depend on them. Pick a service below for the full detail, or contact us if you're not sure which one fits.

Testing & Certify

Live-panel functional testing on every unit, with a condition report and recommendation before it ships back.

Learn more

Circuit Board Repair & Refurbish

Flat-rate repair on control panel cards, roughly 35% of the original purchase cost, done by GE Speedtronic-trained engineers.

Learn more

Spare Parts

New and refurbished replacement boards, including systems the OEM has declared end-of-life, with expedited delivery.

Learn more

Custom Service

A non-standard requirement your plant needs solved? Tell us what it is and we'll work out a way to do it.

Learn more

Remanufacturing

Comprehensive diagnostics, component replacement and re-soldering to restore a board to original specification.

Learn more

Asset Recovery

Upgrading your Speedtronic system? We'll assess and purchase the surplus modules you no longer need.

Learn more
